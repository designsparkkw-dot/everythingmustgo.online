# EMG — Monetization Strategy

## Revenue Streams

### 1. Subscription Plans (recurring, primary revenue)
| Plan | Price | Target | Key Levers |
|---|---|---|---|
| Starter | Free | Individuals/casual sellers | Acquisition funnel — gets users posting, builds supply/liquidity |
| Professional | 19 KWD/mo | Active resellers, small businesses | 50 listings, 5 featured/mo, analytics dashboard |
| Enterprise | 79 KWD/mo | Dealerships, agencies, large retailers | Unlimited listings, homepage banners, API access, account manager |

Billed via KNET (dominant in Kuwait), Visa, Mastercard, Apple Pay.

### 2. Featured Listings & Boosts (à la carte)
- One-off "Feature this listing" purchase (e.g., 1.5 KWD / 3 days) for Starter-tier users who don't want a subscription
- Category spotlight placement (sponsored top-of-category slot)

### 3. Homepage & Category Banner Ads
- Sold to Enterprise businesses and external advertisers (e.g., banks, telcos, real estate developers) targeting GCC consumer intent
- Sold on CPM or flat weekly/monthly placement basis

### 4. Business Directory Premium Profiles
- Enhanced business profile (verified badge, lead capture form, catalog showcase) bundled into Enterprise, sellable standalone to non-listing businesses (services, agencies)

### 5. Lead Generation Fees (future)
- For high-intent verticals (Real Estate, Jobs, Business Opportunities) — charge per-qualified-lead or per-application fee to agencies/recruiters rather than flat subscription

### 6. Transaction Facilitation (future, optional)
- Optional "Secure Pay" escrow service for high-value items (vehicles, electronics) — EMG takes a small % fee for holding payment until buyer confirms receipt

## Pricing Philosophy
- Keep free tier generous enough to win supply-side liquidity (sellers) — classifieds marketplaces live or die on inventory depth
- Monetize intent and visibility (featured placement, banners) rather than gatekeeping basic usage
- GCC expansion: price in local currency with regional parity (KWD baseline, adjusted for SAR/AED/QAR purchasing power)

## Unit Economics Targets (illustrative, validate with real data)
- Target: 3-5% of active sellers convert to paid plans within 90 days
- Target: Featured-listing attach rate of 8-12% of Starter-tier listings
- Blended ARPU target by Month 12: 2.5-4 KWD per monthly active seller

## Go-to-Market Tie-In
- Launch promo: first month of Professional free for early business sign-ups (supply-side seeding)
- Referral incentive: free featured listing credit for successful seller referrals
- Vertical partnerships (auto dealers, real estate brokerages) onboarded directly onto Enterprise with custom terms during pre-launch
